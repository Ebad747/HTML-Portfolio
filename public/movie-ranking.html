The Best Movies According to Ebad

My top movies

Bad Boys

Amazing chemistry between Will Smith and Martin Lawrence, great action scenes and comedy.

Rush Hour

Jackie Chan and Chris Tucker have a great dynamic and leads to the movies humor and is overall entertaining.

Snow White and The Huntsman

The classic folktale of Snow White with a twist and different perspective on it, about war and power over the kingdom with a stepmother who is corrupted by it. Snow White escapes while in danger of her stepmother and is compelled to be brought back by a huntsman who soon feels for her and lets her go.

Hunger Games

Katniss Everdeen volunteers as tribute for her sister Prim at the hunger games a festival where two people man and a women from each of the twelve districts compete to be champions and bring glory to their respective districts. Katniss will soon learn there is someone pulling the strings.
